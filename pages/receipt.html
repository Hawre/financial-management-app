Receipt Journal Guidelines

The Receipt Journal is used to record money received from customers, clients, or other sources. Each receipt affects at least two accounts:

  • Debit: The asset account where the money is deposited (Cash, Bank Account, etc.)
  • Credit: The revenue or receivable account that explains the source of the funds

Common Receipt Scenarios

Scenario Debit (Increases) Credit (Increases)
Customer pays cash for a product Cash Sales
Customer pays for a service via bank transfer Checking Account Service Revenue
Customer pays an outstanding invoice Cash/Bank Account Accounts Receivable
Interest received on investment Cash/Bank Account Investment Revenue

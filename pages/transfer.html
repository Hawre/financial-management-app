Transfer Journal Guidelines

The Transfer Journal is used to move funds between accounts. Each transfer affects two asset accounts:

  • Debit: The asset account where the money is being transferred to (increases)
  • Credit: The asset account where the money is being transferred from (decreases)

Common Transfer Scenarios

Scenario From Account (Credit) To Account (Debit)
Transfer from checking to savings Checking Account Savings Account
Withdraw cash from checking Checking Account Cash
Deposit cash to checking Cash Checking Account
Note: Transfer journal entries only involve moving funds between asset accounts. Use General Journal entries for more complex transactions.
